Loan Eligibility

Thank you for considering our loan services. Below are the eligibility criteria for our loan types:

Age requirements: Applicants must be at least 18 years old or above.
Income requirements: Applicants need to have a steady source of income to qualify for a loan. Minimum income levels and stability of income will be assessed during the application process.
Credit Score: A good credit score is required for loan approval. The exact credit score requirement may vary depending on the type of loan which you are applying for.
Employment Status: Applicants must have a stable employment status, which may include full-time, part-time, or self-employment.
Identification: Borrowers need to provide valid identification documents, such as a driver's license, passport, or government-issued ID, for identity verification.
Residency: Applicants do not have to be citizens or permanent residents of the country. We offer loans to those who are in need but are also able to repay the obligated amount.
Collateral: Certain types of loans may require collateral. Acceptable collateral types will be specified during the application process.
