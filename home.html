    Our Mission

    Crimson's mission is to be the most affordable credit provider available, offering loans to everyone who needs them the most right at their fingertips. to save South Africans from the lower to middle classes from becoming debt-trapped due to payday loan sharks' excessive interest rates.

    Our goal is to combat persistent fraud related to identity theft and loans that are applied for and granted using false documentation. The fourth industrial revolution is something we want to address, and we want to make our system and services readily available online. systems that are simple enough for those without much computer experience to operate.
