Personal loan

A personal loan is a type of unsecured loan provided to individuals for various
personal purposes. With a personal loan there is a fixed end date by which
the loan will be paid off. Personal loans may be secured or unsecured.
We as Crimson Cash Loans only provides secured personal loan which requires
some type of collateral as a condition of borrowing.
Personal loan allows you to borrow a lump sum of money to pay for a variety of
expenses and then repay those funds in regular payments.

How a personal loan work?

Our Personal loans are granted based on the borrower's creditworthiness,
income, and other financial factors. When considering a personal loan, it's
helpful to understand how much it may cost. The annual percentage rate (APR)
on a personal loan represents the annualized cost of repaying the loan
based on the interest rate and fees.

What is personal loan used for?

Borrowers mostly use Personal loan for home renovations or repairs, medical
expenses, wedding expenses, moving expenses, vacations, unexpected
expenses, funeral costs, or other major purchases.

What is a payment method of personal loan?

Our Personal loan are mostly repaid in fixed monthly instalments over a specified
deadline of your pay of, and interest rates can be fixed or variable depending on the
borrower's credit profile. Crimson Cash loans allows a minimum of R1500 up to
a maximum of R500 000 on Personal loan, and the amount you get will
depend on your credit score. Our interest rates are starting from 15% up to
25%, the rate you get will depend on the term of your repayment.
