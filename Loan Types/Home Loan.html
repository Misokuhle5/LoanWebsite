Home loan

A home loan is a contract between a borrower and a lender that
allows someone to borrow money to buy a house, apartment, condo, or other liveable property.

What is the payment method of Home loan?

Crimson Cash Loan's home loan is typically paid back over a term of 10, 15 or
30 years. Our interest rate is starting from 15% up to 25% depending on your payback period.

What is mortgage insurance?

Crimson Cash loans accepts Mortgage insurance. Mortgage insurance can help
homebuyers get an affordable, competitive interest rate and more easily qualify for
a loan with a down payment as low as 3%. In exchange for these better terms, our
borrower pays insurance premiums each month usually for at least several years.
Mortgage insurance also helps Crimson Cash Loans when borrowers default on
home loan, the mortgage insurance company will reimburse your us a percentage
of the amount you owe. Mortgage insurance essentially compensates for the down
payment you didn't make if the Crimson Cash Loans has to foreclose. Crimson Cash
Loans require a down payment of 20% as a condition of qualifying for a mortgage
since a borrower(you) who invests their own money in their home is less likely to give
up on making payments and let the bank foreclose on the home if their home's
value drops or their personal finances deteriorate.
